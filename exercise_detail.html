Barbell Curl

Description

The barbell curl is a classic isolation exercise used to build strength and size in the biceps. It targets the biceps brachii, brachialis, and brachioradialis. Focus on maintaining a stable core and avoiding any swinging of the upper body during the movement.

Target Muscle Biceps
Sets 3-4
Target Reps
8-12
repeat

Personal Notes

"Keep elbows pinned to your sides. Squeeze at the top of the movement for 1 second. Control the descent (eccentric) for a 3-count."
